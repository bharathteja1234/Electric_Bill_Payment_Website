Website Description:

The website is designed to streamline the electricity bill payment process. Administrators can securely login, update the bill calculation calculation formula, manage billing cycles, and calculate fines for late payments. Customers can register and log in to view their bills, calculate the amount due based on their electricity consumption, and make payments using their preferred payment method. The system enforces the specified billing rates based on the type of electricity connection (domestic or commercial) and provides a simple, efficient platform for hassle-free bill payments.
